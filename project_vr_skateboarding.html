Unity VR-Skateboarding

Proof of concept

Skateboarding with hands as feet. Everything object is physics based or in Unity terms has a rigidbody on it. This raises a problem of how feet should follow the physical hands. Essentially shoes/feet are "trying" to achieve the vr-controllers Transform by applying force themselves. This means that shoes act as pure physics objects and will collide with any other Collider.
